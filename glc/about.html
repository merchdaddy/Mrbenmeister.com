About Gold Label Club

Who We Are

A Circle, Not a Brand

Gold Label Club is a private society dedicated to the art of the cigar, the craft of curated experiences, and the value of exceptional company. We are not a venue, a subscription, or a marketing campaign. We are a circle — carefully assembled, discreetly maintained.

Founded by Benjamin Meister, the club began as a private gathering of discerning professionals who believed that the finest experiences belong to those who can truly appreciate them. What started as a monthly smoke in New York has grown into a national and international society with chapters visiting the world's finest cigar establishments.

Every event is intentional. Every lounge we visit is reviewed with the same rigor. Every member earns their place.
